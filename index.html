WHY JOIN US

Oldest College MUN Conference

BITSMUN is the oldest college MUN conference in India. The conference was founded in 2007, and BITSMUN 2018 will mark its tenth edition. Over the years, the conference has attracted college and school delegates from over 15 countries and all across India. The college is also famous for its humble yet iconic campus, and that itself is an experience of its own.

Cashless Committees

BITSMUN is the only MUN in India simulating cashless committees at this scale. Introduction of cashless committees is an attempt to change the incentive of MUNs as a means to win cash prizes, to rather, a means to learn and grow. Since everyone participating in such a committee has no underlying agendas, the quality of debate is considerably higher.

Good Cause

The proceeds from BITSMUN 2018 will be donated for a good cause, after expending towards the necessities for the event. If you have a cause you are passionate about and want us to donate towards, please mail us at secretary.general@bitsmun.co.in
